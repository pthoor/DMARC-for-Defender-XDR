# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We are committed to a respectful, professional, and inclusive project community.

Expected behavior:
- Be respectful in language and tone.
- Assume good intent and provide constructive feedback.
- Focus on ideas and technical outcomes, not individuals.
- Welcome diverse viewpoints and experience levels.

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or unwanted sexual attention.
- Doxxing, intimidation, or threats.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after moderator guidance.

## Scope

This applies to project spaces, issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community interactions related to this repository.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate this policy.

For serious or repeated violations, maintainers may temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers listed in MAINTAINERS.md.
