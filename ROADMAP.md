# Roadmap

This roadmap tracks the high-level direction of DMARC Analyzer Azure.

## Near term

- Complete P1 detection tuning and suppression primitives.
- Expand operational runbooks and deployment validation coverage.
- Strengthen CI security checks and release automation.

## Mid term

- Add adjacent protocol monitoring (TLS-RPT, MTA-STS, BIMI eligibility).
- Introduce sender/provider enrichment and curated classification data.
- Improve executive reporting and digest automation.

## Long term

- Prepare migration path to Defender XDR Advanced Hunting graph functions.
- Expand posture and visibility features while keeping privacy constraints.
